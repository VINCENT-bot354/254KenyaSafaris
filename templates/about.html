254 Kenya Safaris was born from a passion for Kenya's incredible wildlife and a desire to share the magic of this beautiful country with travelers from around the world. Our name "254" represents Kenya's international dialing code, symbolizing our deep connection to this magnificent land.

We believe that every safari should be more than just a trip - it should be a transformative experience that connects you with nature, wildlife, and the rich cultural heritage of Kenya. Our team of experienced guides and safari coordinators work tirelessly to ensure that your adventure exceeds your expectations.
